7 Movies That Make Us Nostalgic

Where have the good times gone? I think about that question often. There was a time in which society lived a more simple life. Grateful for the present moment. In a sense, the simple life is the naive life children live prior to the stressors that come with growing up. Nostalgia brings us back to this life, and it is all around us. One of my favorite ways to experience this feeling is to remininsce in media from my childhood. Below are 7 movies that make us nostalgic.
