7 Must-Try Restaurants in St. Louis

I’ve lived in St. Louis my entire life, and I can say firsthand that we have a great restaurant scene. From local Italian restaurants on The Hill to hole-in-the-wall restaurants scattered throughout the suburbs, there will always be something for you to curb your hunger in this city.

Katie's Pizza & Pasta

For a casual fine-dining experience and unbeatable taste, Katie’s is the place to go. No matter what you choose on the menu, you will never go wrong.

14171 Clayton Rd, Town and Country, MO 63017

(636) 220-3238

Visit Website

Anthonino's Taverna

Home of what some say is the best toasted ravioli in the city, Anthonino’s on The Hill serves delicious Italian cuisine.

2225 Macklind Ave, St. Louis, MO 63110

(314) 773-4455

Visit Website

Billy G's Kirkwood

Not only does Billy G’s have delicious food and drink, but the atmosphere makes it one of a kind. Located in Downtown Kirkwood with a large patio, this is the perfect spot for a night out with friends.

131 W Argonne Dr, Kirkwood, MO 63122

(314) 984-8000

Visit Website

Mom's Deli

Craving a good sandwich? Look no further than this St. Louis legacy. Mom’s Deli serves “great food at a great value.”

4412 Jamieson Ave, St. Louis, MO 63109

(314) 644-1198

Visit Website

Sugarfire Smoke House

Sugarfire has some of the best barbecue in town. Focused on reinventing barbecue, this restaurant takes old traditions of famous barbecue cities to create a unique St. Louis-style BBQ.

605 Washington Ave, St. Louis, MO 63101

(314) 797-8487

Visit Website

Faraci Pizza

Using 100% provel cheese, this St. Louis style brick oven pizza joint serves some of the best za I have ever had.

520 S Florrisant Rd, Ferguson, MO 63135

(314) 524-2675

Visit Website

YaYas Euro Bistro

In my eyes, Yayas Euro Bistro is famous for their mouthwatering babaganoosh, but this restaurant brings much more to the table. The menus are sophisticated, the food is authentic, and the story of Ya Ya brings it all full circle. Don’t miss out.

15601 Olive Blvd, Chesterfield, MO 63017

(636) 537-9991
